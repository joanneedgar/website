In The Beginning

End of page

As a child, I loved being in the kitchen with my mother, aunties and grandmother. They never 'shooed' me away, but encouraged me, by allowing me to contribute during meal preparations. My curiosity grew to include other talents along with cooking and baking. I now enjoy a multitude of hobbies, such as cedar bark weaving, jewelry making, some sewing, building, gardening, fishing and forging, and making medicines, just to name a few.

Kitselas Elders & Dancers 1995

Biography

My name is "Su-Win-A-Ex " which means "Mother Grouse Feeding Her Young." My English name is JoAnne Edgar. I am a mother of 4 girls and Grandmother of 11. I am a member of the Kitselas Band, which is located in Terrace, B.C. and part of the Tsimshian Nation, I am from the Killerwhale Clan and the House of T' ax' ayee.

I was born in Terrace, BC. I have four brothers and three sisters. I have multiple cousins, neices and nephews. Our mother is our matriarch, she has 4 brothers and 3 sisters. She also has multiple cousins, neices and nephews, grandchildren, and great grandchildren.
